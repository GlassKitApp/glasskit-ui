## When to use

DirectionArrow and MapView are two different jobs, not a light vs heavy version of one thing:

- **DirectionArrow** points at one target and nothing else: a friend, your car, the gate, an off-screen Pin, the last 50 m to a door. A single arrow that swings to stay locked on as you turn your head. Zero requests, sensor-only (`useGeolocation` + `useDeviceOrientation`), instant.
- **MapView** gives spatial context: the route, the cross-streets, where you are among options. A real Leaflet basemap (about 42KB plus tiles).

If the wearer only needs a heading, the arrow beats a map: it is lighter, glanceable, and reads as guidance floating in the world. Reach for [MapView](/docs/components/map-view) the moment "which way?" becomes "show me the route."
