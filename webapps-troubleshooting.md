Overview
This page will be updated with known issues, workarounds, and updates as they come up.
When something isn’t working:
Ensure your glasses and Meta AI app are updated to the latest versions.
Try describing the issue to an AI assistant to see if it can resolve the issue.
Test your app on a desktop browser using arrow and enter/return keys. Use your browser’s developer tools for deeper investigation.
